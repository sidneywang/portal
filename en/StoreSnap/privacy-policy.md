# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated**: May 27, 2025

StoreSnap (hereinafter referred to as "this App") respects and protects the privacy of all users. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information.

## 1. Information Collection

This App does not collect any personally identifiable information.

- This App does not require an account or login. We do not collect your name, email address, phone number, or any other personally identifiable information.
- This App does not collect usage data. We do not track how many times you open the App, how long you use it, or your click behavior.
- This App does not collect location information and does not request any location permissions.

## 2. Use of Information

Since this App does not collect any information, there is no use, analysis, sharing, or sale of your personal data.

## 3. Local Permissions

To provide its core functionality (opening the LLM homepage with a global keyboard shortcut), this App may request the following system permissions:

| Permission | Purpose | Required |
|---|---|---|
| Accessibility / Input Monitoring | To detect your custom global shortcut from anywhere on your Mac | Yes |

You can revoke these permissions at any time in System Settings. If you disable them, the shortcut feature will stop working, but other features will remain unaffected.

## 4. Third-Party Services

This App does not integrate any third-party SDKs (such as Umeng, Firebase, Google Analytics, etc.) and does not send any data to third parties.

## 5. Data Storage

All App settings (such as your custom shortcut key combination) are stored locally on your device and are never uploaded to any server.

## 6. Children's Privacy

This App does not knowingly collect any information from users of any age, including children under 13.

## 7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

If this Privacy Policy changes, we will notify you by updating this document in a future App update. The revised policy will take effect on the date of publication.
