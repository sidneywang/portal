# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated**: July 8, 2026

AImger ("the App") respects and protects the privacy of all users. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information.

## 1. Information Collection

The App does not collect any personally identifiable information.

- No account creation or login required; we do not collect your name, email, phone number, or any other identifying information.
- No usage data collected; we do not record your app usage frequency, session duration, or click behavior.
- No location data collected; we do not request any location permissions.

## 2. Network Data Transmission

The App sends photos to the OpenRouter AI platform via HTTPS for classification:

- Photos are transmitted as base64-encoded data in request bodies, solely for obtaining classification results.
- Transmitted content only includes the image files you select for classification, never personal files, browsing history, passwords, or other sensitive information.
- You may choose not to use classification and use local browsing and management features only.

## 3. Local Permissions

To provide core functionality, the App may request the following system permissions:

| Permission | Purpose | Required |
|------------|---------|----------|
| File access (user-selected folders) | Read your selected photo and video folders for classification | Yes |
| Network access | Connect to OpenRouter API for AI image classification | No (offline mode: classification unavailable) |

You can revoke these permissions at any time in System Settings.

## 4. In-App Purchases

The App processes purchases through the Apple App Store. We do not process or store your payment information.

## 5. Third-Party Services

The App uses the OpenRouter API for AI image classification. The API Key is stored locally on your device only, transmitted only in request headers, and never logged or uploaded to other servers.

## 6. Data Storage

- App settings (category labels, language preferences, model selection) are stored locally on your device only and never uploaded to any server.
- Credit balance information is encrypted and stored locally or in iCloud, accessible only on your devices.

## 7. Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect information from users of any age, including children under 13.

## 8. Changes to This Policy

If this Privacy Policy changes, we will notify you by updating this document in the app update. The revised policy takes effect upon publication.
