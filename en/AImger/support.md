### FAQ

**Q: How do I get started?**

Click "Choose Folder", select a folder containing photos or videos, then click "Start Sorting". The AI will automatically identify and classify each image.

**Q: What does classification cost?**

Each classification consumes credits. 1 Credit = $0.0001. New users receive 5,000 free credits.

**Q: Which formats are supported?**

Supports JPG, JPEG, PNG, GIF, WebP, HEIC, HEIF for images, and MP4, MOV, MKV, AVI, WebM, M4V for videos.

**Q: What happens to my files?**

After classification, files are moved into subfolders named by their classification category. We recommend backing up before classification if you want to preserve the original layout.
