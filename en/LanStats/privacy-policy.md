# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated**: July 5, 2026

LanStats (hereinafter referred to as "this App") respects and protects the privacy of all users. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information.

## 1. Information Collection

This App does not collect any personally identifiable information.

- This App does not require an account or login. We do not collect your name, email address, phone number, or any other personally identifiable information.
- This App does not collect usage data. We do not track how many times you open the App, how long you use it, or your click behavior.
- This App does not collect location information and does not request any location permissions.

## 2. Network Data Transmission

This App transmits device performance metrics (CPU usage, memory usage, disk usage, network traffic) over your **local area network only** via TCP, for the purpose of sharing resource usage across multiple machines:

- All data is transmitted exclusively within your local network; nothing is sent to the internet.
- Transmitted content **does not include** any personal files, browsing history, passwords, or other sensitive information.
- Data is transmitted without encryption because all data is anonymous performance metrics and contains no personally identifiable information.

## 3. Use of Information

Since this App does not collect any personal information, there is no use, analysis, sharing, or sale of your personal data.

## 4. Third-Party Services

This App does not integrate any third-party SDKs (such as Umeng, Firebase, Google Analytics, etc.) and does not send any data to third parties.

## 5. Data Storage

All App settings are stored locally on your device and are never uploaded to any server.

## 6. Children's Privacy

This App does not knowingly collect any information from users of any age, including children under 13.

## 7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

If this Privacy Policy changes, we will notify you by updating this document in a future App update. The revised policy will take effect on the date of publication.
